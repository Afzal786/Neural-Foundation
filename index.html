The Neural Foundation is a leading organisation set up to accelerate and support positive change in the Indian education system and beyond.

  • Set up industrial training centres and create jobs in order to stop migration of the teenage youth to metropolitan cities.
  • To provide technical training and training centre of Computer Software, Hardware, Electronics, Electricals, Radio, Television, Mobile, Painting to unemployed educated boys and girls belonging particulars to poor and minority & weaker section of the society.
  • To provide vocational training, small scale, industrial training, cutting edge industrial training, khadi and village industrial training to the rural area women, unemloyed youth, men, handicapped people.

Neural Foundations aims to help poor students and increase the standards of the education in rural areas. Industrial training and new job opportunities are the primary goals.

Why Support Neural Foundations?

It aims at supporting the needy peoples, rising awareness about the education and environment. It promotes the sustainable form of development.
